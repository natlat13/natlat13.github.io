Site Purpose

UmbrellaOrNot.com is a fun and creative site to get your weather forecast. It gives you the current and predicted weather conditions and lets you know if it will be an umbrella kind of day or not by providing a simple umbrella image on a specific days forecast. A gray/blue umbrella means rain, while a yellow umbrella means sun - you may still want to use an umbrella that day. Users will be able to view the conditions of the day by specific times, highs and lows, and at the moment conditions. They will also be able to check future predicted conditions in the same manner.
